ANDROBIOME - Clinical Applications and Sampling

Clinical Indications

  • Androbiome Screen: Screening and differential diagnosis of acute urogenital diseases
  • Androbiome (Full): Chronic forms, ineffective treatment, reproductive function assessment, IVF preparation
  • Couple examination with Femobiome for infertility workup

Preparation Requirements

  • Sexual abstinence or barrier contraception for 3 days
  • Avoid antiseptics and antibacterial soaps
  • Avoid urinating 1.5-2 hours before urethral sampling

Results Interpretation

  • Traffic light color coding: green (normal), yellow (moderate), red (abnormal)
  • Quantitative values with relative proportions
  • Automated conclusion generation
  • Assessment categories: normal composition, moderate disturbance, severe disturbance

Complementary Tests

  • HSV1/HSV2/CMV herpesviruses
  • HPVQuant-21 for papillomaviruses
  • BacScreen OM for urinary tract infections

Multiple PCR tests can be conducted using a single biomaterial sample. Consult your laboratory for availability.
