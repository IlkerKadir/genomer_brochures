ENTEROBIOME KIDS - Children's Gut Microbiota Analysis

Reagent kit for analysis of gut microbiota composition in stool samples from children by real-time PCR. Detects taxa covering 99.9% of total prokaryotic mass.

What It Detects

  • Actinomycetota: Bifidobacterium spp. (child and adult species), Coriobacteriia
  • Bacillota: Cl. leptum gr., F. prausnitzii, Lachnospiraceae, Lactobacillaceae, Streptococcus spp.
  • Bacteroidota: Bacteroides, Prevotella, Parabacteroides, Alistipes spp.
  • Verrucomicrobiota: Akkermansia muciniphila
  • Opportunistic: C. difficile (tcdA, tcdB), C. perfringens gr., Enterobacterales, S. aureus (mecA), S. agalactiae (srr2)
  • Candida spp., Methanobacteriaceae

Key Capabilities

  • Normocenosis/dysbiosis status determination
  • Normal microbiota diversity estimation
  • Bifidobacteria species-level identification
  • Bacillota/Bacteroidota ratio calculation
  • Pathogenicity and resistance markers detection

Kit Features

  • Multiplex format with internal control
  • Automatic results generation
  • Preset templates for PCR instruments
  • Biomaterial: feces (including meconium)
