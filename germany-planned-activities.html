DESCRIPTION OF PLANNED ACTIVITIES IN GERMANY

1. Purpose and Scope

Genomer Biotechnology R&D Industry and Trade Ltd. Co. (hereinafter "Genomer") is preparing a structured market entry into the European Union with its proprietary in vitro diagnostic (IVD) qPCR kits. Within this framework, Genomer plans to establish a liaison office (Verbindungsbüro) in Berlin, Germany. The liaison office will not engage in any commercial or sales activity. Its sole purpose is to carry out promotional, scientific relationship-building, market research and regulatory coordination activities.

2. Legal Status and Framework

The office shall be structured as a liaison office without independent legal personality in accordance with the German Commercial Code (HGB) and applicable regulations. It will act solely on behalf of the parent company, Genomer Biotechnology R&D Industry and Trade Ltd. Co., and will limit its activities to representation and preparatory work. The following commercial activities are explicitly excluded from the scope of the office:

  • No direct product sales, order intake or invoicing will be conducted in Germany or any other EU member state.
  • The office will not place products on the EU market, will not appoint distributors and will not sign direct commercial contracts at the liaison-office level.
  • No commercial transactions giving rise to tax liability will be performed; the office will not generate revenue.

3. Planned Activities

During the transition period until the IVDR 2017/746 conformity assessment is completed, the liaison office will carry out the following activities aimed at building credibility and awareness for Genomer's products in the German and broader European markets:

3.1 Scientific Relationship Building and KOL Engagement

  • Establishing scientific dialogue with leading German university hospitals and research institutions — including Charité (Berlin), Technische Universität München and Universität Heidelberg — and with Key Opinion Leaders (KOLs) in women's health, urology and gastrointestinal microbiome research.
  • Organising one-to-one scientific meetings and presentations to introduce the scientific rationale, clinical performance and supporting publications of Genomer's product portfolio.
  • Identifying and exploring potential clinical research, validation study and joint-publication opportunities with German academic and clinical partners.

3.2 Promotional and Marketing Activities

  • Representation of the company at relevant congresses, symposia and professional events held in Germany and the wider European area (e.g. DGGG, MEDICA, ECCMID, EAU). Any product- or poster-level presentation will be subject to completion of the IVDR certification beforehand.
  • Preparation and dissemination of scientific promotional materials (brochures, monographs, white papers) in German and English to targeted professional audiences.
  • Communication of the technical and clinical benefits of the product portfolio through the company website, peer-reviewed publications and professional online channels.

3.3 Market Research and Strategic Preparation

  • Mapping the structure of the IVD market, reimbursement mechanisms (e.g. GKV / GOÄ codes) and the laboratory chain landscape in Germany and target EU countries.
  • Pre-screening of potential distributors, clinical laboratory partners and key accounts to support the future Phase 2 commercial structure.
  • Collection of competitive intelligence and pricing benchmarks.

3.4 Regulatory and IVDR Process Coordination

  • On-the-ground coordination of the IVDR 2017/746 certification process with European stakeholders (Notified Body, clinical evaluation experts, regulatory consultants).
  • Monitoring of EUDAMED registration requirements, post-market surveillance obligations and the evolving European regulatory landscape, with regular reporting to headquarters.

4. Product Portfolio Covered by Promotional Activities

The Berlin liaison office will conduct scientific promotion exclusively for the following product families, all of which are developed and manufactured by Genomer Biotechnology and currently undergoing IVDR 2017/746 certification:

All listed products are developed by Genomer Biotechnology and manufactured in its ISO 13485-certified production facility in Türkiye. No product sales will be conducted in Germany until IVDR certification has been completed.

5. Operating Model

During Phase 1, the liaison office will not employ local personnel. All activities will be carried out directly by the management team and scientific advisors based at Genomer's headquarters in Türkiye. The Berlin office will serve as a registered address and meeting infrastructure; the team will conduct activities through regular visits to Germany, scheduled meetings and digital communication channels. All operating expenses (rent, travel, events, promotion) will be covered by the parent company's budget.

6. Phase 2 — Roadmap to GmbH Conversion

Following completion of the IVDR 2017/746 Notified Body certification — currently anticipated within approximately 12 months — the liaison office will be converted into a GmbH with its own legal personality, enabling the start of direct commercial activity. The future GmbH is intended to handle product distribution and sales in Germany and other EU member states, manage the distributor network, perform invoicing and, where appropriate, also serve as Genomer's EU Authorised Representative.

This roadmap ensures that Genomer's products will be introduced to the EU market only after all regulatory requirements have been fulfilled and within a fully compliant legal framework.

7. Declaration

Genomer Biotechnology R&D Industry and Trade Ltd. Co. hereby declares that the activities described in this document will be conducted strictly within the scope set out above and in full compliance with German and European Union legislation.
