5.2 SUT (Reimbursement) Strategy

SUT (Sağlık Uygulama Tebliği — Health Implementation Communiqué) is the official regulation issued by Turkey's Social Security Institution (SGK) that governs patient access to healthcare services, treatment reimbursement and payment terms.

Getting our tests included in the SUT list is a strategic priority. Once listed, our tests would be reimbursed by both the SGK (state insurance) and private insurance providers. This would:

  • Significantly increase test volumes at private hospitals,
  • Open the door to public (state) hospitals.

For SUT inclusion, the Turkish Society of Gynecology needs to formally recommend our tests to the Ministry of Health. This is why participation in the TJOD Congress and building relationships with the society's leadership are critically important. Dr. Emanetoğlu's influence in the field will be leveraged throughout this process.
